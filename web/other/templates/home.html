What is password vault?

PasswordVault is a sophisticated programme that allows users to safely store and manage all of their login credentials. This tool can help you create secure, one-of-a-kind passwords for web apps. They are then encrypted and stored in the user's personal vault. To access the saved credentials, users just need to carry one file and remember one password. This programme also includes other capabilities such as the ability to check if any of their accounts have been compromised as a result of a data breach and the option to share passwords with their contacts.

How does password vault works?

PasswordVAULT is easy and simple to use:
1. Register an account: At first you have to register an account, then a datafile will be downloaded. That file will be required for every login, so keep that safe.

2. Login to existing account: After registering an account in the application, login to the app with the username, password and your datafile.
